Hey, I'm Viper!

I've been doing programming since 2020, just messing around with different languages and figuring things out. Then in 2023, I got into Roblox development, which was something I really wanted to do for a while.

Honestly, seeing people create popular games on Roblox was pretty inspiring - I wanted to make something that could get that kind of attention too. So I jumped into Roblox Studio and started learning everything I could.

It's been a really cool journey so far, and I'm doing pretty well with my projects right now. I'm always working on new stuff and trying to get better at what I do. There's something awesome about taking an idea and actually making it work in a game.

I'm still learning every day, but that's what makes it fun. Whether it's solving coding problems or coming up with new features, I'm always down to tackle whatever comes next.

I also create modern, responsive websites with clean design and smooth user experiences. From portfolio sites to business applications, I can bring your web ideas to life.
